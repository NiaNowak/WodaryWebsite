Situated on the edge of Fae's Eyes Peninsula sits Wodary City. A great city of technological advancement and progress.

Wodary City is a place of science and taking a leap for all, its known for;

  • Its unique layout, sporting hexagonal blocks with 3-way intersections, doubled with multiple pedestrian roads.

  • Its unique geography, with a basalt plateaux, drumlins in the north, and sudden flat plains in the south.

  • Sustainability, the science of Wodary is as much for years on as it is for today.

Wodary is never stopping in its projects, this page updates weekly to note our city's top 3 priority projects.

2/6/2024

  1. WTU | Wodary Technological University | 48% complete.
  2. Wodary Park Science Center | 89% complete.
  3. DragonsHead Block infrastructure upgrade | 73% complete.

Wodary has a unique view on contruction, making votes on the designs public to the citizens, making Wodary known as the "City designed by its people", with 83% of the city's population saying they prefer to wait longer for construction to begin if it means as much people as as possible agree on the design being good.
