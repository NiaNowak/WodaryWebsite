Wodary Nation Park is a natural, protected land of wonder and beautiful scenery. It holds rich natural and human history with a multitude of near-extinct species thriving here, the ruins of the O'Mori Castle, a multitude of fairy rings, and more!

Our park introduces a unique ecosystem seen nowhere else, with a multitude of endangered species seeking refuge here, and thriving! Scientists are still trying to find the secret of Wodary Park and what makes it a safe haven for so many species, whether it's scienence, magic, or a mix of both, you can be sure to find its beauty here!
