Wodary, The Dream City.

Wodary is a passion project of mine, a fictional ideal city which sits on the Southern penninsula of a fictional country.

It started when I saw a recommendation on my Youtube feed talking about hexagonal grid cities by "City Beautiful", which lead the algorithim to recommend me more Urban Planning and Urban Design videos. "Not Just Bikes" and "The Aesthetic City" are channel I began to watch a lot, teaching me the basics of pleasant looking, walkable cities.
A lot of these cities are Dutch, leading Wodary to be very Dutch inspired. Wodary sports canals, pedestrian roads and a lot of bike infrastructure.

The world and city are still in progress, but the general geography is thought of. Its somewhat hilly with cliffs and mountains spanning the west coast.

The pale purple and blues are cities and towns, the lime and green are the forest, with the lime being the forest park and the dark green is protected woodlands. The blue lines are rivers while the red are train tracks and stations.

The castle and pink rings in the park relate to the ruins and fairy rings found in the forest.

The sketch to the above is a common look for any inital design for a new animal for the world. Wodary is as much a general world building project as it is a city project. No city is free of animals, may they be strays, domestricated, or just wild animals that realised cities offer a new niche.
